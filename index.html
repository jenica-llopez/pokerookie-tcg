HOW IT WORKS

Air cargo uses the higher of actual weight vs volumetric weight (L×W×H ÷ 6000 for cm/kg). You pay for whichever is greater.

Sea cargo charges by CBM (cubic metres) only — weight doesn't matter. Great for heavy boxes.

Rates are set in the Shipping Rates page. Update them whenever your cargo agent changes their pricing.
